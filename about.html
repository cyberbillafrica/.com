About Us

Welcome to CyberBill Africa

At CyberBill Africa, we are not just a tech company; we're your local source for comprehensive IT support, cybersecurity awareness, consulting, and top-notch training and certification services. Based in the heart of Enugu, Nigeria, we're dedicated to empowering individuals and small businesses with the tools they need to navigate the digital landscape with confidence.

Founded by passionate tech enthusiasts, CyberBill Africa is committed to elevating the technological landscape of our community. With years of industry expertise, our team brings a wealth of knowledge to deliver solutions that are tailored to your unique needs.

Our vision is to catalyze Africa's technological evolution. We envision a continent where individuals and small businesses harness the power of technology to drive innovation, growth, and inclusivity. Through our services and Tech Hub, we aim to empower every African with the skills and support they need to navigate the digital world confidently.

Our mission is to provide unparalleled IT support and security services to individuals and small businesses across Africa. We understand the critical role technology plays in today's landscape, and we're dedicated to ensuring that our clients can operate and innovate without hesitation. We are passionate about bridging the digital skills gap. Our Tech Hub serves as a vibrant learning ecosystem, offering accessible and up-to-date tech education to equip individuals with the tools they need to succeed.
