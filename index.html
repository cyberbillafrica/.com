About Us

Your Trusted Tech Partner

At CyberBill Africa, we empower individuals and businesses with comprehensive IT support, cybersecurity awareness, consulting, and top-notch training. Based in Enugu, Nigeria, we’re dedicated to bridging the digital skills gap across Africa.

Our mission is to provide unparalleled IT and security services while fostering tech education through our vibrant Tech Hub. We envision a tech-savvy Africa where innovation drives growth and inclusivity.
