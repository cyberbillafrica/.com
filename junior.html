3-Month Cybersecurity Bootcamp

From Beginner to SOC Analyst Ready in 3 Months

This intensive 3-month bootcamp transforms beginners into job-ready cybersecurity professionals. Covering foundational concepts, information security techniques, and practical SOC analyst skills, you'll gain hands-on experience with real-world tools and methodologies.

Duration: 3 Months | Focus: Foundational Cybersecurity, Information Security, SOC Analyst Career Path
