Tech Adventures for Kids

Embark on an exciting journey into the world of technology with our fun-filled course designed just for kids. Join us as we explore the magic of digital devices, create amazing art, play with coding, and imagine the tech adventures of the future!

Preparing children for a great future in the Digital World in a fun and engaging way

Getting Started

Course duration: 6 weeks

Course details:

Week 1: Welcome to the Digital World
  • What is technology? Exploring gadgets and devices
  • Introduction to buttons, screens, and touch
  • Fun activities: Drawing on digital devices, playing interactive games
Week 2: Exploring the Magic of Computers
  • Meet the computer: Parts and components
  • Learning about keyboards and mice
  • Fun activities: Interactive typing games, simple drawing software
Week 3: Digital Adventures on the Internet
  • What's the internet? Introduction to websites and browsers
  • Safe online practices: Staying in the kid-friendly zone
  • Fun activities: Online interactive stories, exploring educational websites
Week 4: Creative Fun with Digital Art
  • Discovering digital art tools and apps
  • Creating colorful drawings and patterns
  • Fun activities: Designing a digital masterpiece, making a digital greeting card
Week 5: Let's Play with Coding
  • Introduction to simple coding concepts
  • Creating animations and stories with code
  • Fun activities: Coding puzzles, making a digital animation
Week 6: Tech Adventures of the Future
  • What's next in technology? Exploring robots and smart devices
  • Imagining future tech adventures
  • Fun activities: Building a simple robot, creating a futuristic tech gadget
