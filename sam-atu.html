About me

Welcome

I am Honourable Samuel Chimaobi Atu, currently serving as an honorable member of the Nigerian Federal House of Representatives, where I represent the good people of Enugu North and South Federal constituency. With a deep commitment to public service, I am dedicated to advocating for the interests and betterment of our constituents, fostering positive change, and contributing to the growth and prosperity of our nation.

Education and Background

My early days as a boy have been spent in Umumba Ugwuaji-Awkunanaw, my countryhome. It is both the place of my birth as well as my cradle. I embarked on my academic journey with a fervent desire for knowledge and excellence. Obtaining a bachelor's degree from the prestigious Enugu State University of Science and Technology (ESUT). I have equipped myself with a solid educational foundation that empowers me to make informed decisions and engage in thoughtful discourse on matters of national importance.

Professional Journey

Before entering the realm of politics, I thrived as a successful real estate developer. This valuable experience allowed me to grasp the intricacies of business, development, and community engagement. My background in real estate has endowed me with a unique perspective, emphasizing the importance of sustainable growth and infrastructure enhancement for the betterment of our constituents. Prior to this period, I was also a Technical Adviser on rural areas to the governor of Enugu State. That is one of the positions I have held in government which I am convinced that I put in my best. I worked closely with the Hon. Onyinye Ugwu, the House of Assembly member for Enugu South (Rural).

Vision and Values

My vision is rooted in a commitment to inclusive governance, transparency, and accountability. I am driven by the principles of service, unity, and progress. As your representative, I strive to bridge the gap between policy and people, working tirelessly to address the challenges and aspirations of our community.

Engagement and Impact

Throughout my tenure, I am championing initiatives that promote social welfare, economic empowerment, and equitable opportunities for all our people. By actively engaging with local stakeholders, community leaders, and fellow citizens, I am dedicated to fostering an environment of dialogue, collaboration, and collective growth.

Together, let us forge ahead on this transformative journey, making strides toward a brighter future for Enugu North and South Federal constituency and the entire nation.

Thank you for your unwavering support and trust.
