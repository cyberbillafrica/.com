Course Introduction

Are you ready to embark on a journey into the world of technology? In our course, "Tech 101 for Adults," we invite you to discover the vast and exciting realm of modern technology. Whether you're new to technology or looking to enhance your skills, this course is designed to provide you with a friendly and approachable introduction to the digital landscape.

Tech 101: Getting Started

Course duration: 6 weeks

Course Overview:

In this course, you'll delve into the essentials of technology, step by step. We'll guide you through the foundations of using devices, navigating software, and understanding digital concepts. From setting up your devices to exploring online tools, you'll gain the confidence to embrace technology in your daily life.

What you will learn

  • Basics of using computers, smartphones, and tablets
  • Navigating common software and applications
  • Exploring the internet and staying safe online
  • Understanding digital communication and social media
  • Introduction to online tools for productivity and entertainment
  • Tips for troubleshooting and seeking help when needed
  • Career Opportunities in Tech
  • And lots more

Why should you buy this course

Our course is tailored to the needs of adults who want to bridge the gap between themselves and technology. We understand that learning technology can sometimes be overwhelming, but with our supportive approach and hands-on activities, you'll be well on your way to becoming tech-savvy. By the end of the course, you'll have a solid foundation to confidently navigate the digital world.

Who should Enroll

  • Adults who are new to technology and want to learn the basics
  • Individuals seeking to enhance their digital skills
  • Individuals seeking to switch into Tech
  • Those who want to feel more comfortable using computers, devices, and online tools

Join us in this course and embark on a learning adventure that will empower you to embrace technology with confidence. Get ready to unlock new opportunities in the digital age!

Detailed Course Curriculum

Week 1: Introduction to Technology and Devices
  • Understanding the importance of technology in the modern world
  • Exploring different types of devices: computers, smartphones, tablets
  • Setting up and basic navigation of devices
  • Hands-on practice: Using a computer, smartphone, and tablet
Week 2: Navigating Software and Applications
  • Introduction to common software applications (word processing, email, web browsers)
  • Exploring mobile apps and their functionality
  • Hands-on practice: Writing a document, sending an email, browsing the internet
Week 3: Digital Literacy and Online Safety
  • Basics of digital literacy: understanding URLs, web search, and online resources
  • Online safety and privacy: Recognizing phishing, protecting personal information
  • Hands-on practice: Conducting a web search, identifying safe websites
Week 4: Digital Communication and Social Media
  • Introduction to digital communication tools: email, messaging apps
  • Exploring social media platforms and their benefits
  • Hands-on practice: Sending an email, setting up a social media account
Week 5: Online Tools for Productivity and Entertainment
  • Discovering online tools for productivity (Google Docs, cloud storage) and entertainment (streaming services, online games)
  • Basics of online shopping and e-commerce
  • Hands-on practice: Creating a document in Google Docs, exploring streaming services
Week 6: Tech Career Options and Future Steps
  • Exploring various career paths in Tech
  • Exploring online resources and communities for tech support
  • Reflecting on progress and setting future goals for continued tech learning
  • Final Examination and Grading
